A guide to get you started

Step-01

Register yourself on Finofii Webapp

At Finofii webapp, get yourself registered within few simple steps. Enter your personal details like email address and password that will make you good to go with our advance AI based fund management software.

Step-02

Connect your Current Account

After registration our system will ask you to connect your current account details in our portal. As soon as you connect your current account with any of the bank out there, you will be able to use our software.

Step-03

Get your Payables Streamlined

With the help of our Calendar and automated Statement Analyzer, your payables, inflow, and cashflows will be managed and streamlined in a very efficient manner. Now you don't need to worry about your liabilities, we will.

Once you are the member of Finofii family, our support team will be there for you at any kind of support required for your funds or your business/startup.
