About us

We are Finofii.
Connected finances for Business Owners/Founders.

Our mission is to simplify finances, to improved cashflows for millions of Indian businesses and Founders. We are a technology company that builds trusted, useful and insightful platforms for our founders to run their business finances and improve their relationship with money.

"Finofii" is different for everyone.

As cashflows are different for different business, thus we understand your need and help you to manage your payables and receivables.

Your business deserves financial peace of mind.

That's where we come in. We're here to simplify overly-complex expenses. To help you set goals and give purpose to your business finances. To build peace of mind into your venture's financial future.

We offer the best of both worlds.

We do that by combining easy-to-use, automated investing technology with great analytical tools so you can make your Business/Startup what you want it to be.
